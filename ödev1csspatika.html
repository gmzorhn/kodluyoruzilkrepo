Definition of Sociology:
Sociology is a branch of science that deals with the relationships between people and societies.
Sociology is a branch of social science that systematically examines social changes and transformations at local and level;
contributes scientifically to comprehensive social solutions, the production of social, political and cultural policies, and attempts to plan orderly social life.
